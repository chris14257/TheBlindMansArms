December Craft Workshops

Wednesdays and Saturdays · 3pm–6pm · Max £40 per person including materials and refreshments

Throughout December we host craft workshops on Wednesdays and Saturdays, 3pm–6pm. Each session keeps within a maximum of £40 per person, including tuition, materials and a festive snack and soft drink.

Wiltshire Wreath-Making

Wednesday 4 December · 3pm–6pm

Build a festive wreath using greenery, berries and seed heads inspired by local hedgerows and woodland. All materials supplied.

£35 per person

Book wreath-making (4 Dec)

Hand-Poured Winter Candle Workshop

Saturday 7 December · 3pm–6pm

Blend and pour your own soy candles in fragrances like Winter Fir, Mulled Spice and Orange Clove. Leave with two candles to keep or gift.

£35 per person

Book candle workshop (7 Dec)

Ceramic Bauble Painting

Wednesday 11 December · 3pm–6pm

Personalise ceramic baubles with glazes and underglaze pens. Decorate three or four baubles ready for firing and collection the following week.

£38 per person

Book bauble workshop (11 Dec)

Artisan Chocolate & Truffle Crafting

Saturday 14 December · 3pm–6pm

Temper chocolate, roll ganache truffles and create flavoured bars using local cream, honey and seasonal spices.

£40 per person

Book chocolate workshop (14 Dec)

Natural Soap & Bath Salt Gift Sets

Wednesday 18 December · 3pm–6pm

Blend soothing soaps and bath salts scented with local lavender, herbs and honey. Take home a three-piece self-care gift set, boxed and ribboned.

£38 per person

Book soap workshop (18 Dec)

Woodland Carving: Butter Knives & Ornaments

Saturday 21 December · 3pm–6pm

A gentle introduction to safe carving using locally sourced green wood. Craft a butter knife or a simple hanging ornament.

£40 per person

Book carving workshop (21 Dec)

Upcycled Textiles: Cosies & Keepsakes

Saturday 28 December · 3pm–6pm

Turn remnant tweed, wool and cotton into mug cosies, coasters or small patchwork ornaments – a calm, creative afternoon between Christmas and New Year.

£30 per person

Book textiles workshop (28 Dec)

No Workshop on Christmas Day

We pause our craft sessions on Christmas Day – the pub will be busy serving Christmas lunch instead. You'll find the festive menu on the Christmas Day page.
