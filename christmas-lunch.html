Christmas Day Lunch

A locally sourced festive feast at The Open Eyed Man · £60 per person (food only)

Christmas Day is all about generous plates, familiar flavours and a few small surprises. Our menu celebrates Wiltshire farms and producers, with options for meat-eaters and vegetarians alike.

Arrival

  • Warm spiced apple cordial with cinnamon
  • Honey-rosemary bread with whipped salted butter

Starters

Choose one:

  • Wiltshire game terrine, cranberry gel, toasted brioche
  • Roasted parsnip and celeriac soup, truffle oil, sourdough
  • Smoked chalk-stream trout, pickled cucumber, dill crème fraîche

Mains

Choose one:

  • Free-range bronze turkey, chestnut and herb stuffing, roast potatoes, heritage vegetables, rich turkey gravy
  • Slow-roasted beef rib, thyme jus, Yorkshire pudding, duck-fat potatoes, winter greens
  • Butternut squash, sage and hazelnut wellington, Wiltshire blue glaze, roasted roots (v)

Desserts & Finish

Desserts – choose one:

  • Traditional Christmas pudding with brandy cream
  • Apple and Calvados tart with vanilla custard
  • Local cheese selection with crackers and chutney

To finish:

  • Handmade chocolates
  • Freshly brewed coffee or loose-leaf tea

Guide price: £59 per person for food, within a £60 per head budget (excluding wine and other drinks).
