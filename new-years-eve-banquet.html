New Year’s Eve Banquet

Four-course celebration with drinks included · £120 per person

Ring in the New Year with a banquet that leans heavily on local producers, generous pours and relaxed, unhurried service. The ticket price includes an arrival cocktail, paired drinks with your meal and a midnight toast.

Arrival & Grazing

  • Welcome cocktail: Winter martini with local vodka, apple and rosemary
  • Charcuterie and local cheese platters with pickles and artisan breads
  • Roasted nuts with winter spices

Starter

  • Seared scallops with cauliflower purée and smoked bacon crumb
  • Vegetarian option: Roast cauliflower steak with hazelnut crumb (v)

Served with a glass of local white wine or a non-alcoholic elderflower fizz.

Main Courses

Choose one:

  • Venison loin, red wine jus, beetroot textures, potato fondant
  • Herb-crusted cod, mussel and saffron velouté, winter greens
  • Wild mushroom and truffle pithivier, parsnip cream, wilted greens (v)

Each main is paired with a curated glass of wine, beer or a well-made non-alcoholic alternative.

Dessert & Midnight Toast

  • Dark chocolate torte with salted honeycomb, crème fraîche
  • Alternative: Poached pear with spiced syrup and vanilla mascarpone

At midnight:

  • Glass of English sparkling wine or non-alcoholic sparkling pressé
  • Late-night cheese and biscuit board for the table

£120 per person including arrival cocktail, paired drinks, midnight toast and table water.
